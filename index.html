Nicole Alexa the Undergrad Student

4th year in Chemistry with a part time job

Nicole is in her last year of undergrad who appreciates spending time with friends when she is not studying or at work. Her favorite activities are usually spent with her friends whether it be dinner, hikes, or trying new things. Although she is usually calm and fun to be around, keeping up with school, work, and her social life can be stressful.

Goals

  • Make enough money to pay for her groceries each month
  • Pass all her classes so that she can graduate
  • Spend as much time with her college friends as she can before she graduates and leaves her college town

Frustrations
